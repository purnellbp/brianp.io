Brian Purnell ©

Born in Atlantic City, NJ, the son of a carpenter and school teacher, Brian is a dedicated professional with diverse experiences as a Software Developer, Wireline Engineer, and a proud USMC veteran. Merging technical expertise with military discipline, he has made significant strides in his career.

Joining the US Marines at the young age of 18, he served as an avionics technician at HMX-1, working for both President Clinton and President Bush during his tenure. After dedicating 13 years to the service, he ventured into the private sector, continuing to hone his skills.

As a Wireline Field Engineer at Integrity Wireline, LLC, he has been working full-time since May 2022, with a focus on Gas, Petroleum, Microsoft Word, Oilfield, and Object-Oriented Programming (OOP). He also took on part-time responsibilities as a Software Developer at G3 Contractors from October 2021 to October 2022.

His experience as a Salesforce Developer with Silotech Group, Inc in San Antonio, Texas, from September 2020 to October 2021, further expanded his technical prowess. Before that, he held a Senior Field Supervisor role at GR Energy Services in the Odessa/Midland, Texas area from November 2017 to April 2019.

In 2020, he completed a full-stack web development program at Codeup , obtaining a Certification of Completion in Full Stack Web Development. Codeup is a renowned, fully-immersive, and intensive 20-week full-stack Java career accelerator that provides students with over 670 hours of expert instruction in software development.

Brian currently lives in Boerne, Texas with his wife Rachel, his son Jacob, his daughter Estelle.
